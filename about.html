OUR STORY

Built On Trust, Backed By Experience

RR Teen Set Solution is a trusted name in the construction and fabrication industry, based out of Gurugram, Haryana. We specialize in industrial shed construction, steel structure fabrication, roofing solutions and custom fabrication work for industrial, commercial and residential clients.

Over the years we have completed a wide range of projects — from large industrial warehouses and steel-frame sheds to roofing and finishing work — always with a focus on quality materials, skilled workmanship and timely delivery.

Every project we take on, big or small, is handled with the same commitment: build it strong, build it right, and build it to last.
